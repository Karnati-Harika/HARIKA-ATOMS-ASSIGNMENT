About Shinchan

Shinchan (Crayon Shin-chan) is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Yoshito Usui. It features the everyday life and hilarious mischief of 5-year-old Shinnosuke Nohara and his family in Kasukabe, Japan.

The series is known for its humor, quirky animation style, and unforgettable characters.

First aired in 1992, it remains one of the most popular and longest-running anime series in the world!
