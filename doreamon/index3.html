About the Cartoon

Doraemon is a popular Japanese manga and anime series that started in 1969. It features a robotic cat named Doraemon who travels back in time from the 22nd century to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi.

Doraemon uses amazing futuristic gadgets from his four-dimensional pocket to solve Nobita's problems, but things often turn out unexpectedly! The cartoon teaches lessons about friendship, creativity, and responsibility.

The series has inspired generations of children and is widely loved across the globe.
