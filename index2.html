DESCRIPTION ABOUT CARTOON

This is the website about the characters involved in the cartoon Doraemon. Doraemon first appeared in 1969 and has since become a cultural icon in Japan and many other countries. The series follows Doraemon, an earless robotic cat from the 22nd century, who travels back in time to assist Nobita Nobi, a kind-hearted but lazy and unlucky boy. Doraemon uses various futuristic gadgets from his four-dimensional pocket to help Nobita solve problems, although their adventures often lead to humorous and unexpected outcomes.

The manga spawned a media franchise. Three anime TV series have been adapted in 1973, 1979, and 2005. Additionally, Shin-Ei Animation has produced over forty animated films, including two 3D computer-animated films, all of which are distributed by Toho. Various types of merchandise and media have been developed, including soundtrack albums, video games, and musicals. The manga series was licensed for an English language release in North America via Amazon Kindle. The anime series was licensed by Disney for an English-language release in North America in 2014, and LUK International in Europe, the Middle East and Africa.

Doraemon was well received by critics and became a hit in many Asian countries. It won numerous awards and sold over 300 million copies worldwide. The character of Doraemon was appointed as the first "anime ambassador" by Japan's Foreign Ministry in 2008.
