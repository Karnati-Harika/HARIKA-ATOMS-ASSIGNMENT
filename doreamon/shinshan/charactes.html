Meet the Characters

Learn about Shinchan, his family, and friends! Each character brings their own unique humor and heart to the story.

  • Shinchan Nohara – The cheeky, naughty boy who dances into trouble.
  • Himawari – His jewel-obsessed baby sister.
  • Misae – Shinchan’s strict but loving mom.
  • Hiroshi – The laid-back, hardworking dad.
  • Kazama – The intelligent and often annoyed friend.
  • Bochan – Quiet and loyal, always by Shinchan’s side.
